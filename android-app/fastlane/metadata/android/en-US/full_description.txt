Color Split is a relaxing sorting puzzle where you bring colorful liquids together in the correct bottles.

Tap a bottle, then choose where you want to pour. You can pour a color only into an empty bottle or onto the same color. Complete the level by making every bottle contain a single color.

FEATURES
• Easy-to-learn touch controls
• 100 solvable levels with increasing difficulty
• Hints that perform a valid move
• Undo your previous move
• Add an empty bottle when you need help
• Level rewards and a coin system
• Local weekly leaderboard
• Smooth pouring animations and vivid colors
• Progress stored on your device
• Offline support for core gameplay

Color Split is free to play. Optional rewarded ads may grant bonus coins and require an internet connection. In-game coins are used only inside the game and have no real-world monetary value.
